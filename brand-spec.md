# Brand Spec: Atelier Modern

## Visual Direction: Editorial
- **Philosophy:** Digital Minimalism for High Fashion. Museum-exhibit clarity.
- **Tone:** Calm authority, fluid intentionality, luxury magazine aesthetic.

## Tokens (OKLch Mappings)
- `--bg`: `oklch(98% 0.004 250)` (Surface: #f9f9ff)
- `--surface`: `oklch(100% 0.002 95)` (Paper feel)
- `--fg`: `oklch(15% 0.02 240)` (On-Background: #161c27)
- `--muted`: `oklch(48% 0.012 240)` (Slate/Neutral)
- `--border`: `oklch(15% 0.02 240)` (Sharp Primary/Black borders)
- `--accent`: `oklch(52% 0.10 28)` (Reserved for Highlights)

## Typography
- **Display/Headlines:** `Bodoni Moda`, serif, large, breathing room.
- **Body:** `Hanken Grotesk`, sans, generous line-height (1.6).
- **Labels:** `Hanken Grotesk`, uppercase, 0.15em tracking.

## Layout & Posture
- **Corners:** Sharp (0px).
- **Borders:** 1px solid Primary/Black.
- **Radii:** 0px.
- **Grid:** 12-column desktop (64px margins), 4-column mobile (16px margins).
- **Gaps:** Large (120px section gaps).
- **Shadows:** None. Depth via tonal layers and 1px outlines.
